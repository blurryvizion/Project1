About This Blog

Made as a blog to keep track of Coffee Shops I've visited during my time at Cal State Northridge. I'm hoping to exapnd on this list as I finish up my last year here on campus.

Understanding the Rating Scale

  • 0 — Bad Coffee and Service
  • 2-4 — "Does the job i guess"
  • 5 — Wouldn't go back unless invited to go
  • 6-9 — "My friend needs to check this place out"
  • 10 — Adding to Regular coffee shop rotation
